Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your Privacy, Our Priority

Neuronia is designed with absolute privacy in mind. Here is our complete privacy commitment:

Data Collection

  • No personal information is collected
  • No user identity, name, email, or phone number is requested or stored
  • No behavioural, psychological, or cognitive profiling data is transmitted
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is gathered

Data Storage

  • All game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device only
  • Data is stored using on-device storage and never leaves your device
  • You can delete all stored data at any time from the Settings screen within the app

Network & Internet

  • No internet connection is required to use Neuronia
  • No data is sent to or received from any server during gameplay
  • No external APIs, AI services, or cloud services are used
  • The app functions 100% offline

Third Parties

  • No data is shared with any third party
  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs are integrated
  • No social media SDKs or login services are used
  • No in-app purchases or payment processing is involved

Children's Privacy

  • Neuronia is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages
  • No age verification is required because no personal data is collected
  • Compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Compliance

Neuronia adheres to international privacy standards including GDPR (EU), PDPA (Singapore), CCPA (California), and COPPA (Children's Privacy). Since no personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted, Neuronia exceeds the requirements of these regulations.
